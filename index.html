Ciao

Find your Arduino over Bonjour and talk to it from your iOS device.

Ciao makes it easy to talk to your Arduino from your iPhone or iPod touch. Simply,

  1. Get the app (coming soon)
  2. Load an example sketch
  3. Say hello to your Arduino

Find your Arduino automatically

Typing in IP address or ports? That's doing it wrong. Ciao uses Bonjour, so Arduinos appear the moment they come online and join the local network, even if they use DHCP.

Define the UI from the Arduino

Every Arduino is different, so they should have different UIs. Call Ciao.addDisplay() and Ciao.addButton() from your Arduino code and your iOS device will lay out your LCDs and buttons.

Fast, simple communication

Ciao chats to your Arduino in a line-based, event-driven manner. You can type directly into the app to send messages to your Arduino. The console view also displays exactly what is sent back and forth.

How does it work?

Find out more on the Ciao GitHub Project page, where you can download the Arduino and (optional) Processing code and read the documentation.
